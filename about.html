I am a creative individual passionate about creating 3D environments and interested in learning new techniques. I am comfortable with self learning and adapting to new standards. Additionally, I am capable of working under constraints. Skills that I have include Unity 3D, Maya, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, Paint shop Sai, In Design, Flash, and Solidworks. Furthermore I am also fluent in English, Mandarin Chinese and Japanese.

As an individual, I am fascinated by East Asian culture and often draw inspiration from my travels to these places. I am also interested in working with the concept of immersive environments and have done projects where the focus is specifically on creating engaging 3D environments.
