A Midsummer Dream

This was an individual project and I created everything from scratch.

The location of this scene is inside a bedroom on a summer afternoon. The light is the room is on but the sunlight shining through the window is still brighter. There is a bed, a desk, a bookshelf with books and a mat in the room. The feeling that I am aiming for in this scene is a simple bedroom in a warm midsummer lazy afternoon. I try to achieve this by giving the lights yellow and orange tints to simulate an afternoon and warm feeling.
