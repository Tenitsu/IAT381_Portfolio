Projects

Finding Alice

Finding Alice is an immersive 3D environment project created in Unity 3D. Maya was used to create all the models in the 3D environment. My contribution includes the creation of the Bedroom, Clock Tower, Crossroad, Card Chase and the final revelation scene. I also manipulated the sounds and their timing for the game. Furthermore, I am also the debugger for the game as a whole and the project manager.

A Midsummer Dream

A midsummer Dream was a 3D environment project that I did. I created models in Maya, applied texture and played around with lighting. Specific attention is paid to lighting detail and rendering of the environment.
